About Crust & Crums

Where passion meets flavor — and every bite tells a story.

Our Journey

Crust & Crums was officially established on 11th October 2024 by Syed Muhammad Atid & Sons. Our journey began with a humble home kitchen, driven by the love for fast food that’s fresh, tasty, and made with care. What started as a small dream quickly turned into a local favorite, thanks to our commitment to quality and authentic flavors.

From crispy burgers to cheesy pizzas and sizzling BBQ, every item is crafted with premium ingredients, attention to detail, and a touch of love. Our goal has always been simple: make food that makes people happy and keeps them coming back for more.

We’re not just serving food — we’re creating experiences where taste, aroma, and freshness come together in every bite.

Why Customers Love Us

  • Fresh & Quality Ingredients: Only the best for our burgers, pizzas, and BBQ items.
  • Flavor-Forward Recipes: From spicy sauces to creamy malai boti, our taste never compromises.
  • Fast & Reliable Service: Hot, fresh food delivered right to your door.
  • Passion & Dedication: Every dish reflects our love for cooking and serving our community.
